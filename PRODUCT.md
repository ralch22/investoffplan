# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Off-plan property investors researching Dubai/UAE — a mix of UAE residents and international buyers (India and UK are the priority international segments; rupee/pound price framing matters to them). They arrive from search with a concrete question ("sold prices in X", "is Y a good developer", "how does off-plan work") and are comparison-shopping high-stakes purchases. Secondary: the site's own advisors receiving leads.

## Product Purpose

investoffplan.com is a Dubai off-plan research platform: 1,700+ project pages, community guides, DLD sold-price data, developer comparisons, and an AI advisor — built to rank for off-plan/investment queries and convert research sessions into qualified WhatsApp/callback leads. Success = organic traffic on money keywords + lead volume, earned through genuinely better data than the portals.

## Brand Personality

Authoritative, data-grounded, editorial. The research authority with magazine-grade presentation: DLD-numbers-first rigor (Bloomberg energy) delivered with premium editorial confidence — serif display, breathing imagery, data as the hero. Red is a signal, never decoration. Honesty is a brand feature: methodology caveats, min-sample gates, and "not this developer's own sales" hints are rendered, not hidden.

## Anti-references

- **Generic Dubai agency glossy**: gold accents, skyline stock heroes, WhatsApp buttons scattered everywhere, superlative-soup copy ("luxury living redefined").
- **AI-startup slop**: purple gradients, glassmorphism, hero-metric templates, eyebrow-kicker scaffolding on every section, identical icon-card grids.
- (Portal-familiar patterns — cards, filters, listing grids — are acceptable where they serve the research job; the site should not fear resembling a serious tool.)

## Design Principles

1. **Data earns the drama.** The boldest visual moments belong to real numbers (DLD medians, spreads, price trends) — never to decoration.
2. **Honesty rendered, not buried.** Caveats, sample sizes, and provenance are part of the visible design language, styled with care rather than hidden in footers.
3. **Editorial confidence.** Serif display speaks once and clearly; restraint elsewhere. One committed red; disciplined neutrals; dark surfaces for hero/footer framing.
4. **Bilingual is first-class.** Every surface ships EN + AR (RTL) with equal polish; the Arabic experience is never a mirror-afterthought.
5. **Research momentum.** Every page answers its query fast, then offers the next question (related comparisons, communities, sold prices) — depth without dead ends.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA (established by the repo's own audits and mobile a11y test suite: tests/mobile/a11y-touch.spec.ts). Touch targets ≥44px, keyboard-reachable interactions, contrast at AA on both light and dark surfaces, `prefers-reduced-motion` honored, full RTL support for Arabic.
